Factors influencing the documentation of quality requirements in agile software development

Time constraints, the limited awareness about quality requirements (QRs), and communication gap among team members influence documentation of QRs in ASD.


Allocation of enough time, good QR awareness among stakeholders and good communication among team members support optimal QR documentation.

  • Time constraints: refer to the limited time available due to the short iteration cycles of ASD.

  • Limited awareness about quality requirements: refers to the lack of knowledge regarding QRs.

  • Communication gap among team members regarding quality requirements: refer to miscommunication regarding QRs that can occur among ASD team members.
